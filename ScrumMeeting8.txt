Minutes from Previous Meeting
This is the minutes for the SCRUM Meeting 8 for Omicron, held on April 28, 2023. The attendees were Max Hadley, Yash Dalal, Will Whatley, and Shicong Chu. The meeting began with each attendee sharing their progress from the previous day and their plans for the day ahead. They also discussed any roadblocks they were facing. The team then reviewed the sprint goal, which was to have a fully functional UI that integrates an AWS database with manager, server, and customer side functionality. They discussed any tasks that needed to be pushed out in order to reach the goal and demoed the website's capabilities on Render. Next, the team discussed areas of improvement and identified tasks that needed to be moved up in priority for the sprint. During the status update, the team shared their accomplishments, which included fully integrating back-end database tables with the UI, implementing the Google Translate API, and deploying the website on Render. The team then discussed their plans for the next meeting, which included finalizing the ordering UI, connecting it to the back-end and order history table, and implementing dynamic resizing of the website to fit screen size. Task assignments were made, including navigating to the cashier page, inputting customer orders, confirming and recording all customer orders, restarting the current order, placing an order and having it confirmed for pick-up, restarting the order, resizing the website to fit the device's screen, and removing underperforming items. The meeting concluded with a reminder of the tasks assigned and the goal for the next meeting.
